Zone B
中华文化展示区
Chinese Cultural Showcase · B1–B18
B

区域介绍 · About

中华文化展示区由 希望、美中、哈维、载道、卓教国际 等社团合作呈现，分为两组：B1–B10 位于场地西侧，B11–B18 位于场地东侧。涵盖围棋、剪纸、书法、陶笛、簪花、贵州民族服饰等中华传统文化与非物质文化遗产，以及教育推广和有奖互动活动。

Jointly presented by Hope, Mei Zhong, Harvey, Zai Dao, and Zhuojiao International. B1–B10 on the west side, B11–B18 on the east. Activities include Weiqi (Go), paper cutting, calligraphy, ocarina, Fujian hair flowers, Guizhou ethnic costumes, intangible heritage demonstrations, and educational outreach.

特色项目 · Featured Activity

中国文化节长卷绘画活动 · Long-Scroll Painting Activity (H6)

自 2023 年起，文化节每年举办现场绘画活动。2024 年恰逢中美建交 45 周年，活动采用 45 尺长卷形式，寓意两国关系走过 45 年，此后长卷每年增加一尺。长卷绘画色彩丰富、参与自由，深受各族裔青少年喜爱，是文化节最具人气的互动项目之一。

今年新增 "各族裔共画中国龙" 环节，旨在突出中国文化元素、增强视觉冲击力，以龙为纽带促进多元文化交流。

Since 2023, the festival has hosted a live painting activity. In 2024 — the 45th anniversary of US–China diplomatic relations — a 45-foot scroll was used, with one foot added each year since. The activity is colorful, open to all, and beloved by youth from every ethnic background. This year introduces "Painting the Chinese Dragon Together" — a multi-ethnic collaborative segment celebrating cultural exchange.
