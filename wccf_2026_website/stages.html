02 · Two Stages, All Day Long

两大舞台

From awe to participation, two parallel worlds of performance.

Elite-level programming on the Main Stage runs non-stop throughout the day. Meanwhile, the 2nd Stage invites the audience to step off the sidelines and into the spotlight — line dances, ping pong matches, and shared joy.
