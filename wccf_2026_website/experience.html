Ten Hands-On Stations

十大文化体验站

From intangible heritage crafts to ancient board games — pick up a brush, wear a headpiece, master a movement.

— Station 01
汉服 & 民族服饰
HanFu & Ethnic Attire

Try on traditional ethnic clothing and stunning HanFu displays — see China's 56 cultural heritages up close.

Try On
— Station 02
福建簪花
Fujian Zanhua

Wear intangible cultural heritage floral headpieces — the iconic Fujian flower crowns recognized by UNESCO.

Heritage
— Station 03
传统脸谱彩绘
Festive Face Painting

Get a traditional festive face painting inspired by Peking Opera's iconic character archetypes.

Family
— Station 04
中国书法
Chinese Calligraphy

Pick up the brush in a masterclass — learn from master calligraphers and take home your first character.

Masterclass
— Station 05
剪纸艺术
Paper Cutting

Cut intricate patterns under folk masters' guidance — a 1,500-year-old art form passed down for generations.

Take Home
— Station 06
绒花工艺
Iron-Wire Velvet Flowers

Craft delicate velvet flowers wire by wire — once exclusive to imperial court ladies of the Tang Dynasty.

DIY
— Station 07
中国民族乐器
Traditional Instruments

Listen to, learn about, and even try guzheng, erhu, pipa and dizi — thousands of years of musical heritage.

Music
— Station 08
围棋对弈
Weiqi (Go)

Test your strategy against players of all levels — the 2,500-year-old game of territory and intuition.

Strategy
— Station 09
筷子夹豆赛
Chopstick Bean Race

Test your chopstick mastery in a friendly speed competition — equally fun for first-timers and lifelong pros.

Competition
— Station 10
彩带龙舞
Ribbon Dragon Dance

Pick up a ribbon and join the dragon procession — easy moves, infectious rhythm, unforgettable photos.

Movement
Make the Most of Your Day

三个小建议

01
先取文化护照

Pick up a Cultural Passport at the entrance. Collect stamps from each station — full stamps unlock souvenirs and food discounts.

02
穿着舒适

Plan to be on your feet most of the day. Wear comfortable shoes — the venue spans an entire city block.

03
开启 AirDrop

Professional photographers around the venue will AirDrop you free high-resolution photos. Turn on AirDrop ahead of time.
