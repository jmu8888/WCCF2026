03 · A Culinary Map of China

舌尖上的中国

Taste your way through a continent — province by province.

The festival's massive Food Exhibit is a literal culinary map. Signature dishes from viral, highly-rated local Chinese restaurants come together so you can taste authentic specialties from every corner of the country — all in one afternoon.

食
新疆 Xinjiang 北京 Beijing 四川 Sichuan 上海 Shanghai 蒙古 Mongolia
From One Street, A Whole Continent

从一条街,尝遍一个大陆。

Visitors can taste authentic specialties from various provinces, including the fiery flavors of Sichuan, the savory-sweet notes of Shanghai, and aromatic barbecues from Xinjiang and Mongolia.

From iconic Chinese Bao and artisanal dumplings to savory Chinese Burgers (Roujiamo) and refreshing bubble teas — it's the ultimate "must-eat" checklist for any food lover.

Eight Regional Profiles

八大风味,一站收齐

— Region 01
川菜
Sichuan
Profile · 麻辣 The Fire

Mapo tofu, dan dan noodles, mouthwatering chicken, hot pot with bone-deep mala heat — Sichuan peppercorns deliver that signature numbing tingle.

— Region 02
上海菜
Shanghai
Profile · 甜咸 Savory-Sweet

Soup dumplings (XLB), red-braised pork belly, scallion pancakes, sticky rice balls — delicate, glossy, comfort-food classic.

— Region 03
新疆烧烤
Xinjiang BBQ
Profile · 香辣 Aromatic

Cumin lamb skewers, fluffy naan bread, big-plate chicken, hand-pulled laghman noodles — Silk Road flavors over open flame.

— Region 04
蒙古烧烤
Mongolian BBQ
Profile · 粗犷 Hearty

Grilled mutton, salty milk tea, buuz dumplings, smoky grilled fare from the steppes — robust, deeply satisfying.

— Region 05
面点
Bao & Dumplings
Profile · 经典 Iconic

Pan-fried Sheng Jian Bao, hand-pleated jiaozi, steamed cha siu bao, soup-filled XLB — perfect handheld bites.

— Region 06
肉夹馍
Roujiamo
Profile · 街味 Street Star

Xi'an's iconic flatbread sandwich — slow-braised meats packed into a crispy bun. Some call it the original Chinese burger.

— Region 07
奶茶饮品
Bubble Tea
Profile · 清新 Refreshing

Hand-shaken milk teas, fruit teas, cheese foam crowns, brown sugar boba — the perfect afternoon companion.

— Region 08
点心甜品
Sweets & Pastries
Profile · 甜蜜 Sweet

Egg tarts, mooncakes, candied haw, tanghulu — China's iconic sweet street snacks across generations.
