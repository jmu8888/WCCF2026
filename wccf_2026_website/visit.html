Frequently Asked · 常见问题

出发之前

真的免费吗？需要门票吗？
Yes, completely free. (Free Admission · Open to All) No tickets, no registration required. Just show up. Some food and souvenirs at vendor stalls are paid.
什么时候开始？什么时候结束？
All-day event on Saturday, August 29, 2026. The opening ceremony begins at 10:00 AM and programming runs through early evening. (See the Stages page for a sample running order.)
下雨怎么办？
The festival is held rain or shine. Some performances may relocate to covered areas in inclement weather. Watch the festival's social media for day-of updates.
可以带小朋友吗？适合什么年龄？
Absolutely — the festival is designed for the whole family. Kids will love the face painting, ribbon dragons, chopstick races, and panda mascots. Strollers welcome.
可以带宠物吗？
Service animals welcome. Leashed pets are allowed in outdoor areas, but the venue is crowded and noisy — for your pet's comfort, consider leaving them at home.
怎么领文化护照？
Pick up a free Cultural Passport at the main entrance or any info booth on the day of the event. Collect stamps at each station to unlock souvenirs and food discounts.
那个免费照片是怎么回事？
Professional photographers will be stationed across the venue. They'll capture your moments and AirDrop the high-resolution photos directly to your iPhone — totally free. (Turn on AirDrop in your settings ahead of time.)
